About me

Over the past years I have spent my time developing skills that I am passionate about; finance, skiing, and programming.

After graduating from university I developed my financial knowledge and undertook the ACA at a big 4 accounting firm in London. Upon completing my training contract I decided I needed to make up for lost time (as we weren't allowed any holiday over Winter) and to move to Whistler, Canada for a ski season. There I pushed my limits in big mountain skiing, and coached young athletes in racing, freeride and freestyle.

When Covid-19 hit and the ski season was abandoned, I saw lockdown as an opportunity to pick up a new skill that I had always wanted to do - programming.
